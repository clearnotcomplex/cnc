The Stoics Way

100 insights from Stoic philosophy, organised across four essential topics to help you think and live more clearly.

About the Project

The Stoics Way is a curated collection of 100 insights drawn from the works of Marcus Aurelius, Seneca, Epictetus, and other Stoic philosophers. The goal is to make Stoic wisdom accessible, practical, and immediately applicable to modern life.

Rather than presenting dense philosophical texts, each insight is distilled into clear, actionable guidance organised across four key themes:

  • The Self — Understanding and mastering your own mind
  • Others — Navigating relationships and social life
  • Adversity — Handling challenges, setbacks, and suffering
  • The Good Life — Living with purpose and meaning

Features

The app is designed with clarity and focus in mind:

  • Daily insight delivered each morning
  • Browse insights by theme or philosopher
  • Save favourites to your personal collection
  • Minimalist, distraction-free reading experience
  • Works offline for reflection anywhere

Progress

The project is currently in active development. The content curation is complete, and I'm now focused on building the web application. Expected launch is Q2 2025.

Technology

Built with simplicity in mind: static HTML/CSS with vanilla JavaScript, hosted on Netlify. No frameworks, no build tools—just fast, accessible web pages that work everywhere.
